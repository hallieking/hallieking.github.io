About Me

My name is Hallie King and I am a graduate of the Sam Fox School of Design & Visual arts at Washington University in St. Louis, with a BFA in Communication Design and a minor in Human-Computer Interaction.

My recent professional design experiences have centered around branding, print, and interaction design for the visual and performing arts, and my personal practice covers a broad array of disciplines including book design, illustration, installation, and motion design. This year, I was also featured on the GDUSA Students to Watch list for 2024.

When I’m not designing I can often still be found making something, whether it’s a knit or crochet project, a dessert (I make a great peach pie), or a miniature plein-air gouache painting.

Reach out any time at hallieking525@gmail.com.
