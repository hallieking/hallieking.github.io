To The Lighthouse

Book Jacket Design

This book jacket redesign was a typographic project that involved creating letterforms out of physical materials and photographing them. To achieve the appearance of letter-shaped sandcastles, the letters were constructed using kinetic sand and made to resemble the Fidra typeface.
