Explore

Board Game Packaging Design

This board game packaging project was created for my Word and Image course. For this project, I was given the word “curious” and tasked to create packaging for a product that captured my interpretation of the word. Explore is a trivia board game that teaches children ages 7+ information about outer space. Inspired by mars rover missions, each gamepiece is based on a real NASA rover, and the game board and packaging resemble the terrain of Mars.
