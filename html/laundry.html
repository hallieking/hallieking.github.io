Photography

This project is the culmination of two related projects for my Image and Meaning course. The first assignment involved the creation of a photo grid using everyday objects or settings. The photogrid that I created is pictured on the left and is made up of photos of a laundry basket.

The second assignment challenged me to create a three-poster series related to the subjects in the photogrid. My poster series focuses on the different phases of the laundering process, and features the pictograms used on garment care labels.
