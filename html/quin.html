Quinquagenarian Poster Series
.
Typographic Poster Design

In this project, I was challenged to create a set of three typographic posters, each with a specific set of restrictions. Each posterâ€™s content includes a word from the Oxford English Dictionary along with its part of speech, definitions, and references. My project focuses on the word Quinquagenarian, which refers to a person aged between fifty and fifty-nine years and can be used as both a noun and an adjective.

For all three posters, I was restricted to the use of a single typeface. The second poster allowed only the use of one type weight, and the first poster restricted both type weight and size.

Throughout the project, I decided to focus on the process of aging, including the positive aspects of gaining wisdom and experience, as well as the loss of abilities such as memory, vision and mobility.
