The Tango Dancers
.
Editorial Illustration

This final project for a course titled Pictures for Communication challenged me to create an editorial illustration inspired by the mid-century periodical fiction illustrations of Al Parker.

The details of the assignment called for the inclusion of two or more figures engaged in an activity, however the environment of the scene could only be implied through the use of props, costumes, and setting elements. In order to give the illusion of a performance, I set the two characters under a spotlight and placed a rose at their feet.
