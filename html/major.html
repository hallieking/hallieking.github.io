The Major Arcana
.
Collection Poster

For this assignment from my Word and Image class, I was assigned the topic “The Major Arcana”, and given the task of designing a collection poster.

To create the poster, I decided to focus on three tarot decks from different historical time periods: the Visconti-Sforza deck, created in 1450, the Etteilla deck, created in 1791, and the Waite-Smith deck, created in 1909. In order to emphasize the iconographic development of the cards over time, the poster highlights three cards from each set: the moon, the star, and the sun. Additionally, I settled on a neon-sign-inspired visual language for the poster, mirroring the neon signage often seen advertising tarot readers and psychics.
