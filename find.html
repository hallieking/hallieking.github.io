Find your Style

Motion Graphics and Digital Prototype

Find your Style is a motion graphics project intended to introduce viewers to popular interior design styles. The project consists of three animated videos, each featuring a unique design aesthetic with the goal of helping people curate their ideal living space.

In order to capture the comfort and personalization of designing an interior space, the style of the videos was created using gouache paint as the primary medium. The different furniture components of each of the three rooms were painted separately from the walls of the room, then digitally placed in the space and animated individually.
